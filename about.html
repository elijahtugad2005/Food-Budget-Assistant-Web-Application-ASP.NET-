About Eatsy

Our Mission

Eatsy is a food budget assistant designed specifically for students and visitors around CTU Daanbantayan Campus. We understand the challenge of finding affordable yet satisfying meals on a student budget, which is why we created this platform to help you discover food options that match your budget constraints.

What We Offer

Our web app provides a simple way to search for food based on your budget, explore local restaurants near campus, and make informed decisions about where to eat. We've collected information about various food stalls, canteens, and restaurants in the area to help you find the perfect meal without breaking the bank.

How It Works

Simply enter your budget in the search section, and Eatsy will show you all available food options within your price range. You can also browse through our curated list of local eateries to discover new places to try. Each listing includes essential information like location, menu highlights, and price ranges to help you plan your meals effectively.
